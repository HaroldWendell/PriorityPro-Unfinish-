FAQ's

What is Priority Pro?

Priority Pro is a powerful and user-friendly to-do task app designed to help you manage your daily tasks efficiently. It allows you to organize, prioritize, and track your tasks, ensuring you stay focused and productive.

How can I create a task in Priority Pro?

Creating a task in Priority Pro is simple. Open the app, click on the "Add Task" button, and fill in the task details, such as title, due date, priority, and category. Click "Save," and your task will be added to the list.

Can I set reminders for my tasks?

Absolutely! Priority Pro allows you to set reminders for your tasks. Simply choose a due date and time for the task, and the app will send you a notification at the specified time to remind you of the pending task.
